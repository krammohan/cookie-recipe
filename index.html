A Simple Chocolate Chip Cookie

Take a Quick Survey

Here's a quick little recipe for making delicious chocolate chip cookies for any occasion.

You will need:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 stick unsalted softened butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 bag of chocolate chips

Recipe:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Add in butter and sugar and beat until smooth.
  4. Add in eggs and vanilla and beat.
  5. Add chocolate chips and mix until doughy.
  6. Roll dough into small 2-3 inch diameter balls and place on greased baking sheet.
  7. Bake for about 13 minutes at 375 degrees.
  8. Let cool for as long as you can bear to and then dig in!
